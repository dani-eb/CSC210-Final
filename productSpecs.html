We use an easy to open pull tab can so you don't have to use a can opener. They're always broken anyways. The can is stainless steel with a thin plastic liner as to not mess with the amazing taste. The can is 115.2mm high and 66.3mm wide. With everything at a super light weight of 12oz. The cans are stackable on top of each other which makes for easy storage. Also the contents of the can are amazing and delicious.

The food is the real star of the Chinese Tinner. The food is delicious and nutrious and can replace a full meal. It can be eaten straight from the can or heated up in a skillet for a little bit of browning and enhance the flavors. There is no need for refrigeration and the food never goes bad which makes Chinese Tinner a great option for meal prepping or doomsday prepping.
